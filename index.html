How we work

From idea to high-performing thumbnail in 5 clear steps.

01

You send the inquiry

You reach out with your channel, video, and what you need help with. That gives us the starting point.

02

03

We create the thumbnail

We design the final thumbnail and send it your way. Need changes? Request unlimited revisions until you are 100% happy.
